Why you're losing jobs

Six reasons bathroom fitters
miss out on enquiries

None of these are hard to fix. But they're costing you jobs every week.

Your number isn't obvious
Bathroom customers want to call. If they can't find your number in 3 seconds, they're calling your competitor.
Broken on mobile
Eight in ten bathroom searches happen on a phone. If your site doesn't work on mobile, you're losing before they read a word.
Too slow to load
Three seconds is the limit. If your site takes longer, half your visitors leave before seeing your work.
No photos of your work
Bathroom refits sell on results. Without before and after photos, there's nothing to convince someone you're the right fit.
No idea what you charge
People need a ballpark before they call. No pricing or budget guide means they bounce to someone who answers the question.
No reviews, no trust
People hire who they trust. Without reviews prominently displayed, they choose the fitter who has them.
